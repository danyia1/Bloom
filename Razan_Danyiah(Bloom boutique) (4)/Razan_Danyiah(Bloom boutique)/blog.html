Tulip

The tulip symbolizes new beginnings, with its bright colors shining like the sun. It represents eternal love and renewal, embodying a quiet yet deep beauty, like a fresh start full of hope.

Carnation

The carnation is a flower full of emotion, exuding love and passion. With its vibrant colors, it gently touches the heart and expresses deep appreciation and ever-renewing affection.

Lily

The lily is a symbol of purity and tranquility. Its fragrant white petals bring a sense of inner peace and represent timeless beauty, a symbol of hope and lasting grace.

Daffodil

The daffodil symbolizes new beginnings with its bright yellow color. It represents optimism and joy, reminding us that life always offers new opportunities.

Orchid

The orchid is the flower of distinction, possessing an enigmatic beauty and delicate strength. With its exquisite details and unique colors, it represents luxurious beauty that is rare and unforgettable.

Rose

The rose is the most romantic of flowers, expressing deep love and passion. From red to white, each rose tells a story of affection, devotion, and admiration.
